Apply to be on our 2020 team!


Job Description

As a TEDxUTSC Associate you will work alongside the Executive team in engaging the audience at events and all logistics-related tasks for the Conference. You will become the expert in executing all the needed ins and outs for the day of the event as well as learn the various aspects of organizing a TEDx conference. We are looking for those who are highly organized, eager to learn and interact with TEDxUTSC’s audience.

What we look for:

  • A University of Toronto student for the school year of 2019-2020
  • Excellent time management skills, ability to multi-task, and attention to detail
  • Excellent interpersonal skills and ability to communicate ideas effectively
  • Ability to work independently as well as in a team

By joining the TEDxUTSC Associates team, you are partaking in a long-lasting legacy—a mission to unite ideas worth spreading where tomorrow is created.
